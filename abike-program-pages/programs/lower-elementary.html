Lower Elementary Program

Ages 6–9 dive into big stories of the universe and humanity, then branch into math, language, and culture using concrete materials.

Montessori
Hands-on
Child-led

Curriculum Threads

  • Language: grammar symbols, word study, composition
  • Math: bead chains, stamp game, passage to abstraction
  • Culture: biology, geography, early history timelines
  • Research: question → investigate → present
  • Community: grace, responsibility, collaboration

Daily Rhythm

  1. Arrival & community jobs
  2. Lesson block + work cycle
  3. Snack + outdoor play
  4. Project time & presentations
  5. Reflection journals & dismissal

Outcomes

  • Curiosity and research habits
  • Solid reading, writing & math skills
  • Growing responsibility and leadership

Pre‑School Program

  • Ages: 3–5
  • Focus: Practical Life, Sensorial, early Language & Math
  • Daily Flow: Uninterrupted work cycle, snack, outdoor play
  • Outcomes: Independence, concentration, love of learning

Children move freely through carefully prepared shelves of Montessori materials—pouring, sorting, building, tracing sandpaper letters, and exploring numbers with golden beads.
